JESUS DELA PEÑA
NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Jesus Dela Peña National High School envisions itself as a leading secondary school and e-learning
center, developing learners who are literate, life-long learners, and imbued with values like being
God-fearing, patriotic, humble, servant-learners, and globally competitive. The school’s mission is to
equip students with the knowledge, skills, character, and discipline needed for success in a rapidly
changing society, supported by Information and Communication Technology (ICT). Core values of
Good Character, Discipline, Good Taste, and Excellence guide its commitment to shaping well-
rounded individuals prepared for the future.
